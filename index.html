About

PetConnect is a website with the aim of promoting the adoption of pets who need a house to call their own. The pets that are up for adoption here were chosen through the Patinhas Online website , it may be that some of them have already been adopted (which leaves my heart warm). If you really want a pet, give them a chance and adopt it , don't buy it!
